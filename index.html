Our Expertise

Our Practice Areas

Lex Practica provides comprehensive legal services to a diverse clientele, with a strong focus on both contentious and non-contentious matters. Our practice spans the following key areas.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

  • Representation before Trial Courts, High Courts, and the Supreme Court.
  • Extensive experience in civil, matrimonial, criminal, and white-collar litigation.
  • Handling recovery proceedings, succession matters, attachment, and regulatory disputes under statutes such as PMLA, PBPT, FEMA, Benami, and NDPS.
  • Effective management of complex, multi-jurisdictional cases for individuals and corporates.

Consumer Litigation

  • Advising and representing clients in consumer disputes, including insurance claims and matters under the Consumer Protection Act.
  • Advocacy before District, State, and National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions.
  • Focus on securing timely and practical resolutions for consumer grievances.

Arbitration & Alternative Dispute Resolution

  • Representation of clients in arbitration proceedings and MSME disputes.
  • Advising on pre-arbitral strategy, drafting, and enforcement of arbitral awards.
  • Promotion of cost-effective and timely resolution for commercial and contractual disputes.

Business Law & Advisory

  • Comprehensive support for business setup, contract drafting, and corporate compliance.
  • Drafting and vetting contracts, assisting in commercial negotiations, and conducting legal due diligence.
  • Assistance with registration of documents and entity formation for both new ventures and established companies.

Financial and Institutional Work

  • Empaneled with leading financial institutions such as PNB Housing Finance.
  • Legal retainers for reputed corporates including Muds Management Pvt. Ltd., Solethreads (Summersalt Lifestyle Pvt. Ltd.), and Alpha Instrumentation Pvt. Ltd.
  • Experience in banking, finance recoveries, and regulatory frameworks relevant to business entities.

Our Team

Adv. Rahul Rai

Advocate Rahul Rai brings over a decade of experience to the firm, specializing in attachment proceedings under statutes such as PMLA, PBPT, and FEMA before the Adjudicating Authority and the Appellate Tribunal for Forfeited Property (ATFP). He routinely represents clients in civil and matrimonial disputes across all judicial forums, including trial courts, the High Court, and the Supreme Court. His practice encompasses complex litigation, regulatory compliance, and strategic case management, supporting individuals and businesses in navigating multifaceted legal proceedings.​

Adv. Kajal Keshwani

Advocate Kajal Keshwani has been part of Lex Practica for the past five years. Her expertise includes conducting proceedings under the Consumer Protection Act and the Negotiable Instruments Act, along with significant experience in matrimonial litigation, succession petitions, and diverse civil matters. She regularly handles dispute resolution and has assisted clients in contract drafting and documentation, contributing to successful case outcomes and enhanced legal compliance for both individuals and corporate entities.​

Adv. Manya Goel

Advocate Manya Goel has worked with Lex Practica for three years and focuses her practice on attachment proceedings before the ATFP, consumer litigation, and arbitration matters. She has represented clients before regulatory and quasi-judicial bodies and assisted with business setup, document registration, and drafting commercial contracts. She is known for her thorough preparation and attention to procedural detail, helping clients achieve reliable resolutions in contentious and non-contentious matters.

Adv. Mohit Gupta

Advocate Mohit Gupta has over a decade of experience in criminal litigation and currently serves as an Assistant Public Prosecutor for the Government of NCT of Delhi. He has prosecuted and defended matters before all levels of courts, addressing diverse criminal cases including economic and white-collar offenses. Adv. Gupta is skilled in managing complex investigations and trial procedures, with substantial experience in ensuring fair law enforcement and client representation within statutory and procedural frameworks.
